Business Problem: The Finance Department of a Company maintains the invoice level details of all Payroll vendors and the cost associated with generating payslips for their employees. Invoices are received for each vendor and all the details from each invoice are filled in an excel sheet. These same data are collated in one spreadsheet from various countries. The Global Finance leader and other Country-level Finance leaders seek data to identify the Per Payslip Costs (PPC), which helps them in negotiating with vendors to reduce overall cost for the company.

Dataset Description: Analysis were performed on the following datasets

1. Country-wise Detailed information on Vendor Charges/fees( Regional Table1, Regional Table2)
2. Country-wise Payslip Information (Payslips)
3. Master Mapping table – Country – Region – Vendor Name (Mapping)
4. Summary Table consists of Monthly summary of cost as well as payslip information
